内存的金手指有两种传输线：数据传输线和地址传输线

```assembly
write():
 push   rbp
 mov    rbp,rsp
 mov    DWORD PTR [rip+0x0],0x2        # e <write()+0xe>
    R_X86_64_PC32 a-0x8
 nop
 pop    rbp
 ret
 ```
